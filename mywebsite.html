Tips for finding
available domain names.





Get smart with synonyms.

Try using variations of a word you want in your domain to get more
options. For example, if you want a domain that includes the word
mechanic, also type closely related words, like repair or automotive,
into the search bar.

Mix up word combinations.

Let's take another look at that domain for mechanics. If you can't think
of any catchy synonyms, you could still dream up a few word
combinations that mean the same thing, like fix cars or auto shop.
